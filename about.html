About me

You can call me Kieran, but I go by Viper among my friends and WantedOutlaw on AO3.

I like to write stories and draw, sometimes.

I had an interest in writing since I was a middle school but never wrote anything of substance until I turned 20. I wrote a shitty Skyrim fanfic once (and I lost it) when I was younger, I think it was 20 pages long or something, and as one might expect it was abysmal, to put it lightly.

It was cringe and shit but I remember I had fun wrriting it, so I decided to try again, but this time I wanted to write a whole preject from start to finsih. and as it turned out, I'm actully enjoying it. Hell, I'm loving it.

I never considerd myself to be the artistic or creative type, but I always loved creating things and spent the better half of my childhood trying differant crafts and tinckering with things. Most went no where, but I would be lying if I said I didn't enjoy every moment of it.
